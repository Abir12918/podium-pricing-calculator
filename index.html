How pricing is calculated

Simple monthly pricing logic for Rahat and Abir.

1. Monthly hours = weekly hours × 4
2. Tutor monthly cost = monthly hours × tutor pay per hour
3. Total partner profit = profit per person × 2
4. Parent monthly price = tutor monthly cost + total partner profit
5. Parent hourly rate = parent monthly price ÷ monthly hours
